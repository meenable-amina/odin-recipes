Crispy Chicken

this is a picture of a delicious cripy chicken

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • seasoning
  • flour
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • oil
  • water

Steps

  1. Season the Chicken: Sprinkle the chicken pieces with a little salt and pepper.
  2. Prepare the Stations: Get two bowls. In the first bowl, whisk the milk and egg together. In the second bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, and all the spices.
  3. Coat the Chicken: Dip a piece of chicken into the flour mix first (this helps the liquid stick).
    • Dip it into the milk/egg mixture.
    • Put it back into the flour mix, pressing firmly so the flour clumps up on the surface
  4. Heat the Oil: Fill a pan with enough oil to cover the chicken halfway. Heat it over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry: Carefully place the chicken in the hot oil. Fry for about 4–5 minutes on each side until they are golden brown and crispy
  6. Place the finished chicken on paper towels or a wire rack to remove excess oil
